# Archetype E: Progress / Status Screens

**Status:** 🔄 Transform after Calibration

## Screens
- EarningsScreen
- XPBreakdownScreen
- TaskHistoryScreen
- ProfileScreen
- TrustTierLadderScreen
- TrustTierLockedScreen
- TrustChangeExplanationScreen

## Emotional Contract
> "Value is accumulating."

## Visual Intent
- Quiet power
- Numbers that feel earned
- Progress that feels inevitable
- Clean data presentation
- Celebratory without being loud

## Allowed Atoms
- HScreen (ambient=true)
- HCard (stat containers)
- HStatCard (key metrics)
- HMoney (earnings, prominent)
- HText (clear hierarchy)
- HBadge (tier badges, status)
- HTrustBadge (tier display)
- HButton (secondary actions)

## Forbidden
- ❌ Dashboard overload
- ❌ Too many numbers competing
- ❌ Charts without context
- ❌ "You need to..." language
- ❌ Shame for low numbers

## Earnings Display
- Hero number at top
- Period selector subtle
- Breakdown expandable, not default
- Always positive framing

## XP/Trust Display
- Current tier prominent
- Progress to next shown simply
- Benefits of next tier hinted
- Never feels far away

## History Display
- Cards for each task
- Most recent first
- Money earned visible
- Status shown with badges
- Tap to expand details

## Copy Rules
- "You've earned" not "Total earnings"
- "Level up" not "Next tier"
- "{X} to go" not "{X} remaining"
- Celebrate milestones

## Empty History
Never "No tasks yet"

Instead:
- "Your history starts here"
- Show one example card (grayed)
- CTA to find first task
