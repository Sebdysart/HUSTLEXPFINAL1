- EntryScreen
- LoginScreen
- SignupScreen

## Emotional Contract
> "You've arrived. You're already in."

## Visual Intent
- Ambient orb (system alive)
- Soft purple gradients
- Centered content
- Generous whitespace
- Single focal point per screen

## Allowed Atoms
- HScreen (ambient=true)
- HText (hero, title1 for headlines)
- HButton (primary for main CTA)
- HTextButton (secondary actions)
- HInput, HPasswordInput (auth forms)
- HCard (form containers)
- HAmbientOrb

## Forbidden
- ❌ Empty states
- ❌ "Get started", "Sign up", "Join"
- ❌ Feature lists
- ❌ Tutorial overlays
- ❌ Aggressive CTAs

## CTA Language
- "Let's go"
- "Continue"
- "I'm ready"
- "Welcome back"

## Copy Rules
- Human, warm, confident
- One idea per screen
- No feature dumps
- Understated, not salesy
